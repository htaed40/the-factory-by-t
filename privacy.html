Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

The Factory by T operates MadeOnI24 and related media channels. We use public social platforms, including Pinterest, Facebook, and Instagram, to share products, videos, updates, and project news.

Information We Collect

This website does not collect account passwords, payment information, or private messages. When the owner connects an official social account to an internal publishing tool, the platform may provide account identifiers, profile information, boards, Pins or posts, and performance analytics such as impressions, saves, clicks, reach, and engagement.

How We Use Information

We use platform data only to publish content that the account owner has specifically reviewed and approved, manage content on accounts owned by The Factory by T, and prepare internal performance reports. We do not automate follows, comments, messages, or other engagement actions. We do not publish a Pin without the account owner's specific approval.

Pinterest Data

Our private, first-party Pinterest tool is used only with The Factory by T's own business account. It may read owned-account profile, board, Pin, and analytics data and may create an owner-approved Pin. It does not access third-party accounts, scrape Pinterest, manage advertising, or publish Pins without human review and approval.

Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell personal information or Pinterest data. We do not share platform data with unrelated third parties. Data is used only for The Factory by T's owned accounts and internal business reporting.

Storage, Retention, and Security

Access credentials are stored securely and are not placed on this public website. Platform data is retained only as long as needed for publishing, reporting, account security, and legal obligations. Access credentials and retained platform data are removed when no longer needed or when platform access is revoked.

Your Choices and Data Deletion

The account owner may revoke connected-app access through the relevant platform settings. Questions or deletion requests may be sent to i24merch@gmail.com. We will remove applicable stored platform data unless retention is required for security, legal, or accounting purposes.

Third-Party Platforms

Facebook, Instagram, Etsy, YouTube, Pinterest, and GitHub Pages each operate under their own terms and privacy policies. Links from this site may take you to those platforms.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to i24merch@gmail.com or through The Factory Facebook Page or Instagram profile.
